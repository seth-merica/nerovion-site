The Problem

Most communication
happens without words.

Tone shift
Micro-expression
Engagement dropping
Hesitation

"55% body language. 38% tone of voice. 7% the actual words."

Mehrabian, A. & Ferris, S. (1967). Inference of Attitudes from Nonverbal Communication in Two Channels. Journal of Consulting Psychology, 31(3), 248-252. Note: these ratios specifically apply to communication of feelings and attitudes -- the exact domain Nerovion operates in.

For most people, reading these signals is automatic. For roughly 15 to 20 percent of the workforce, it isn't. Tone. Posture. Microexpressions. The pause before someone answers. These signals arrive constantly in professional conversations, and a significant portion of the working population processes them differently.

A neurodivergent professional can be highly intelligent, deeply skilled, and completely unaware that the client they just pitched is no longer interested. Not because they weren't paying attention. Because the signal that communicates disengagement, a slight shift in posture, a flattening of tone, a glance away at the wrong moment, never registers the same way.

The professional world runs on unspoken rules. Knowing when someone is frustrated before they say it. Sensing that a yes actually means maybe. Catching the moment a conversation turns. These aren't soft skills. They're survival skills. And a significant portion of the working population has spent their entire careers developing workarounds because no tool existed to help.

How It Works

Intelligence delivered
in the moment.

Nerovion runs as a Chrome extension during your Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams calls. No one else sees it. No audio or video is stored anywhere.

01

Connect to Your Call

Nerovion integrates natively with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Google Meet. One authorization. No disruption to your existing workflow.

02

Real-Time Analysis Begins

As your call runs, Nerovion reads facial expressions, body language, tone shifts, and conversational subtext -- across all participants in your session.

03

Discreet Insights Surface

A private overlay visible only to you delivers clear, actionable interpretations. Not jargon. Not data. Real guidance you can use in real time.

04

Build Awareness Over Time

Post-session summaries and pattern tracking help you understand recurring dynamics and measure your growth across every interaction.
